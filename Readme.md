Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI Project)
📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales performance analysis of Blinkit – India’s Last Minute App using Power BI.

The objective of this dashboard is to analyze:

📊 Sales Performance

⭐ Customer Satisfaction

📦 Inventory Distribution

🏬 Outlet Performance

The dashboard provides business insights using KPIs, interactive visualizations, and data-driven storytelling to support strategic decision-making.

🎯 Business Requirement

To conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to identify key insights and opportunities for optimization using various KPIs and visualizations in Power BI.

📊 KPI Metrics

The following key performance indicators (KPIs) are used in this project:

Total Sales – Overall revenue generated from all items sold.

Average Sales – Average revenue per transaction.

Number of Items – Total count of different items sold.

Average Rating – Average customer rating of items.

📈 Dashboard Features & Visualizations
1️⃣ Total Sales by Fat Content

Objective: Analyze the impact of fat content on total sales.

Chart Type: Donut Chart

Insight Focus: Compare Low Fat vs Regular items.

2️⃣ Total Sales by Item Type

Objective: Identify top-performing item categories.

Chart Type: Bar Chart

Insight Focus: Sales contribution by item type.

3️⃣ Fat Content by Outlet (Total Sales)

Objective: Compare outlet sales segmented by fat content.

Chart Type: Stacked Column Chart

4️⃣ Total Sales by Outlet Establishment Year

Objective: Evaluate how outlet age impacts revenue.

Chart Type: Line Chart

5️⃣ Sales by Outlet Size

Objective: Analyze correlation between outlet size and total sales.

Chart Type: Donut Chart

6️⃣ Sales by Outlet Location Tier

Objective: Assess geographic distribution of sales.

Chart Type: Funnel / Bar Visualization

7️⃣ All Metrics by Outlet Type

Objective: Comprehensive performance comparison across outlet types.

Chart Type: Matrix Card

📌 Key Insights Generated

Regular fat products generate higher revenue compared to low fat products.

Tier 3 locations contribute the highest sales.

Medium-sized outlets dominate in total sales.

Supermarket Type1 has the highest total revenue among outlet types.

Sales peaked around 2018 establishment outlets.
